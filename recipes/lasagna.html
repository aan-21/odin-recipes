Lasgna Recipe

Description

Brown meat with onions and bell peppers, and season to taste. Add tomato paste and water. Let simmer. Begin layering all ingredients beginning with a few spoonfuls of tomato sauce. Follow with noodles, then ricotta mixture, and shredded mozzarella. Repeat until dish is filled.

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 onnion, chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper chopped
